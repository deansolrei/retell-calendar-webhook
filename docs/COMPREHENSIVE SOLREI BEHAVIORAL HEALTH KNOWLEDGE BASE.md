=== FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS ===

Q: How quickly can I get an appointment?
A: New patients typically seen within 5-7 business days. Urgent cases may be accommodated sooner.

Q: Do you prescribe controlled substances?
A: Yes, when clinically appropriate and with monthly monitoring. All controlled prescriptions require regular appointments and medication agreements.

Q: What if my insurance isn't listed?
A: Contact us to verify. We may be able to work with your insurance or offer self-pay options with superbills for reimbursement.

Q: Can I see my provider via telehealth from another state?
A: You must be physically located in a state where your provider is licensed during the appointment.

Q: How do refills work?
A: Routine refills processed within 48 hours. Controlled substances require monthly appointments. Request refills through patient portal or pharmacy.

Q: What's your policy on medical marijuana?
A: We don't prescribe medical marijuana but can provide documentation of diagnosis for state programs where applicable.

Q: Can parents attend minor's appointments?
A: Yes, for patients under 18. Adolescents may request private time with provider as clinically appropriate.

Q: Do you offer therapy or just medication?
A: We provide medication management with supportive therapy. For intensive psychotherapy, we can provide referrals.

Q: What if I'm having side effects?
A: Contact us immediately for severe side effects. For mild side effects, we'll address at next appointment or sooner if needed.

Q: Can you fill out disability or FMLA paperwork?
A: Yes, with established patients. Forms completed within 5-7 business days. Fee may apply.
